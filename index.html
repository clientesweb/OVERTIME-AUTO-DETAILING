Frequently Asked Questions

Ceramic coating is a liquid polymer that is applied to the exterior of a vehicle. It chemically bonds with the vehicle's factory paint, creating a layer of protection that enhances gloss and provides long-lasting protection against environmental contaminants.

A full detail typically takes between 4-8 hours, depending on the size of the vehicle and the level of detailing required. For more extensive services like paint correction or ceramic coating, it may take longer.

For optimal maintenance, we recommend having your car professionally detailed every 3-4 months. However, this can vary depending on your driving habits, environment, and how well you maintain your vehicle between details.
